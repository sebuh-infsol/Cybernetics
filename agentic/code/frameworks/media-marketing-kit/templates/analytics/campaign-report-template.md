- [Paid Social strong at first-touch (30%) - good awareness channel]
- [Paid Search strong at assisted (45%) and last-touch (30%) - demand capture]

## 9. Learnings & Recommendations

### 9.1 Key Learnings

**What Worked**:

1. **Paid Search outperformed expectations**
   - [4.2x ROAS vs 2.0x target]
   - [Non-brand keywords delivered 80% of conversions at lower cost]
   - [Recommendation: Increase Paid Search budget for future campaigns]

2. **Webinars generated highest-quality leads**
   - [45% MQL→SQL conversion vs 30% campaign average]
   - [10% registration→MQL conversion rate (3x higher than other channels)]
   - [Recommendation: Make webinars core part of demand gen strategy]

3. **Retargeting delivered highest ROAS**
   - [5.1x ROAS - highest of any channel]
   - [Critical touchpoint in customer journey (40% of conversions)]
   - [Recommendation: Always run retargeting for all campaigns]

4. **Video creative significantly outperformed static**
   - [2.5% CTR vs 1.5% for static images (67% improvement)]
   - [Recommendation: Invest in video production for future campaigns]

**What Didn't Work**:

1. **Display advertising failed to drive conversions**
   - [0 MQLs from $4,900 spend]
   - [0.8% CTR (below 1% benchmark), 60% bounce rate]
   - [Root cause: Generic targeting, no ABM focus, low-intent audience]
   - [Recommendation: Avoid display for future enterprise demand gen campaigns - better for brand awareness]

2. **Paid Social (Meta) underperformed LinkedIn**
   - [Meta: $160 cost per MQL, 1.5x ROAS]
   - [LinkedIn: $133 cost per MQL, 3.0x ROAS]
   - [Root cause: Enterprise buyers not active on Meta, broad targeting]
   - [Recommendation: Focus social budget on LinkedIn for B2B enterprise campaigns]

3. **Email list fatigue in final 2 weeks**
   - [Open rates dropped from 28% to 18%]
   - [Root cause: Too many sends (2-3 per week) without suppression rules]
   - [Recommendation: Implement email frequency caps (max 1 per week)]

4. **Ad fatigue mid-campaign**
   - [Creative CTR declined 40% from week 1 to week 6]
   - [Root cause: Same creative ran for 6 weeks without refresh]
   - [Recommendation: Plan creative refresh cadence (every 4 weeks)]
