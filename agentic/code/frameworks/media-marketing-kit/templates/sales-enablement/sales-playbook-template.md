### Discovery Call Structure

**Opening** (5 min):
- Build rapport
- Set agenda
- Confirm time

**Understand Current State** (10 min):
- {Question 1}
- {Question 2}
- {Question 3}

**Identify Pain** (10 min):
- {Pain question 1}
- {Pain question 2}
- Quantify impact

**Future State** (5 min):
- {Goal question}
- {Success criteria question}

**Process & Timeline** (5 min):
- Decision makers
- Timeline
- Budget

**Next Steps** (5 min):
- Summarize
- Agree on next step
- Schedule follow-up

### Key Discovery Questions

**Current state**:
1. "Walk me through how you currently handle {process}."
2. "What tools are you using today for {function}?"
3. "How many people are involved in {process}?"

**Pain identification**:
1. "What's the biggest challenge with your current approach?"
2. "When {problem} happens, what's the impact?"
3. "How much time/money does this cost you?"

**Future state**:
1. "In an ideal world, how would this work?"
2. "What would success look like 12 months from now?"
3. "What would change for your team if this was solved?"

**Process**:
1. "Who else needs to be involved in this decision?"
2. "What's your timeline for making a change?"
3. "How have you approached similar decisions?"

## Demo Best Practices

### Demo Structure

| Section | Duration | Focus |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Opening | 5 min | Confirm agenda, pain points |
| Overview | 5 min | High-level value proposition |
| Core demo | 20 min | 3 key features tied to pain |
| Differentiation | 5 min | Why us vs. alternatives |
| Q&A | 10 min | Address concerns |
| Next steps | 5 min | Clear action items |

### Demo Rules

1. **Lead with pain**: Reference their specific challenges
2. **Show, don't tell**: Feature → Benefit → Proof
3. **Limit features**: Focus on 3-4 most relevant
4. **Tell stories**: Use customer examples
5. **Check in**: Pause for questions and reactions
6. **Customize**: Use their data/scenarios if possible

### Demo Talk Tracks

**Feature 1: {Feature Name}**

> "You mentioned that {their pain}. This is how we address that..."
>
> [Demonstrate feature]
>
> "What this means for you is {benefit}. {Customer name} saw {result} after implementing this."
