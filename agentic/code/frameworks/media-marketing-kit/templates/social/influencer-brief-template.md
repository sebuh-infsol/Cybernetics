## FTC & Disclosure Requirements

### Required Disclosures

**All content MUST include**:
- Clear disclosure of paid partnership
- Disclosure must be prominent and unmissable
- Must appear within first three lines (Instagram)
- Must be visible without clicking "more"

**Acceptable formats**:
- #ad (required)
- #sponsored
- "Paid partnership with {Brand Name}" (platform feature)
- Verbal disclosure in video content

**Placement**:
- [ ] Beginning of caption/video
- [ ] Above the fold
- [ ] Not buried in hashtags
- [ ] Repeated if content is long-form
